## üí° Pro Tips

### For Best Results:
- **Upload screenshots** of current versions (v0 learns from them)
- **Be specific** about what to keep/change
- **Iterate** - refine with follow-up prompts
- **Test on mobile** - v0 preview shows mobile view
- **Export early** - Don't over-refine before testing

### Common Prompts:
- "Make this more minimal"
- "Add smooth animations"
- "Improve mobile experience"
- "Make CTAs more prominent"
- "Add social proof section"
- "Optimize for conversion"
